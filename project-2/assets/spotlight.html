Spotlight

Jason Potsander

Jason Potsander is a local cyclist and runner who has competed at a high level while battling pancreatic cancer. While as of late his focus has been on cycling races, he is a staple of the running community in the area. He was a member of the Goshen College Cross-Country and Track programs when he attended the school and is still involved with the team. The Cross-Country team renamed their character to honor Potsander and give him a chance to share his story with the athletes.

Potsander Award - The Record

Justin Gillette

Justin Gillette is a marathon runner with an impressive 100 marathon wins in his career. He holds the Goshen College Marathon record and his own personal record is 2:25:44 which averages out to be about 5:33 per mile; super fast! He is still actively running the streets of Goshen often pushing one of his children in a stroller that has logged over 10,000 miles of runs.
